Our Story

Where the
Levant Meets Oslo

Levant Spektrum brings the warmth, spice, and soul of Arabic and Mediterranean cooking to the center of Oslo. Rooted in the culinary traditions of the Levant — Syria, Lebanon, and beyond — every dish tells a story of culture, heritage, and flavor.

We believe great food is a conversation between the past and present, served with heart. Our kitchen is a temple of tradition where ancient recipes are honored with modern precision.
